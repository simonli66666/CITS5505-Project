Points System

Tasty features a rewarding point system, encouraging active participation and valuable contributions from our members. Earn points by sharing recipes, engaging in discussions, and interacting with fellow food enthusiasts!

  • 20 Points
    For signning up.
  • 5 Points
    For posting a new recipe.
  • 2 Points
    For getting a like.
  • 2 Points
    For posting a comment.
  • 2 Points
    For getting a comment.
Badges System

Besides gaining reputation with your shared recipes, you receive badges for being especially helpful. Badges appears on your profile page.

  • Tasting Novice
    10 Points
    The "Tasting Novice" badge is recognition for beginners, indicating that you've started exploring the world of gastronomy. From trying out new recipes to learning basic cooking techniques, you're taking your first steps on a culinary journey.
  • Culinary Apprentice
    50 Points
    The "Culinary Apprentice" badge is recognition for active learners, indicating that you've gained a deep understanding of the basics of cooking. You may have mastered some common cooking techniques and are starting to try more complex dishes.
  • Gastronomic Explorer
    100 Points
    The "Gastronomic Explorer" badge is recognition for explorers, indicating that you've delved into various culinary cultures and cooking techniques. You may have tried a wide variety of ingredients and dishes, and have a deeper understanding of the breadth and depth of cooking.
  • Culinary Master
    200 Points
    The "Culinary Master" badge is the highest honor for outstanding contributors, indicating that you've become an expert in the culinary field and have a profound understanding and influence in the world of gastronomy. You may have become a leading figure in the community, inspiring and guiding other members, and collectively driving the development of culinary culture.
